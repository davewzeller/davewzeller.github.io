Atlantis is a thirty-five-year-old mom with three kids. She is a sales agent for Bass Pro Shop. Her favorite pastime is fishing. She is getting ready to take her kids out and needs to plan for the weather. Keeping kids engaged in fishing is an arduous task.

Ducky McDuckface has been hunting for the past thirty years. He has taught his two boys and three girls how to hunt and track, and duck hunting is his favorite. He owns the Unlucky Ducky boat famous for winning several tournaments. He is getting ready for a game in southern Idaho and needs the weather and other available activities.

Snorkeling Summer is getting ready to take her kids' swimming. She needed to know how warm it is outside and where she can take her kid. He is only four and can get into trouble fast.

Randy Rodeo is looking to take his teenagers to the rodeo. He needs to be able to look up when and where it will take place. He is also hoping that it will be a clear night with no rain in the forecast.

Molly and Joe Norman are heading out for a family hike. With six kids in tow, outdoor activities are some of the few affordable options for family time. They are looking at the weather and opportunities for their next adventure.

Gray Granpa wants to take his two grandkids on a walk by the beach. He gets prepared by checking the weather, gathering suntan lotion and treats. He knows that having the best treats will prove that even prehistoric people can be extraordinary.
