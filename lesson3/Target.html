The Smith Family


The Smith family's parents are trying to get the teenagers outside away from the TV screens. They have teenage kids, two daughters, and two sons. In the last couple of years, the kids have been spending more time on computers and phones. They're concerned that their kids are not experiencing anything outside the little screens. The Smiths want to look up the weather and outdoor activities for the whole family.


Max and Ali


Max and Allie are newlywed couples who don't have a lot of money and even less time to spend. They're looking for low-cost ideas to be able to spend more time together. They were thinking of a picnic or hike or even a free concert. They are deftly fair-weather people who don't want to get too hot or too cold. They're still in the honeymoon phase of their marriage want to be together as much as possible.
